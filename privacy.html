Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Who we are

Lexi Taylor System ("we," "us," "our") operates an automated Instagram creator account (@lexiloverlee) that uses artificial intelligence to generate content and reply to direct messages. The account is openly labeled as an AI Creator on Instagram. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information related to people who interact with our account.

2. Information we collect

When you send a direct message to our Instagram account, comment on a post, or otherwise interact with our content, we may receive the following information from Meta's Instagram Platform:

  • Your Instagram-assigned user identifier (a numeric ID, not your username or real name)
  • The text of messages you send to our account
  • Timestamps of your interactions
  • Public information you've made available on your Instagram profile (display name, profile picture)

We do not collect, store, or process: your email address, phone number, home address, payment information, government identifiers, or any other personally identifying information beyond what Instagram provides to authorized business accounts.

3. How we use information

Information we receive is used solely to:

  • Generate contextually relevant replies to your direct messages
  • Remember the conversation history so future replies can reference what you've previously discussed
  • Track basic engagement (whether you've interacted recently, content topics you've engaged with) for the purpose of providing relevant content
  • Detect distress signals and route those conversations to a human reviewer with appropriate resources

4. AI-generated content disclosure

All replies sent from our Instagram account, and the majority of visual content posted to the account, are generated by artificial intelligence. The Instagram account is labeled as "AI Creator" per Instagram's AI labeling feature. If you sincerely ask whether you are speaking to a human or AI, our system will respond truthfully.

5. What we don't do

  • We do not sell, share, or trade your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not use your data to train external AI models
  • We do not generate or store psychological profiles of individuals
  • We do not contact you outside of Instagram
  • We do not attempt to meet in person, request your phone number, or solicit personal contact information

6. Data retention

Message history is retained for as long as you remain an active subscriber/follower of the account. If you block our account, unfollow, or do not interact for 180+ days, your conversation data is archived. You may request deletion of your data at any time via the data deletion endpoint or by emailing us (see Section 9).

7. Data security

Conversation data is stored on encrypted servers operated by Railway. Communication with Instagram and the language model providers (Anthropic) is conducted over HTTPS. We use industry-standard practices for access control and key management.

8. Third parties we use

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — to send and receive messages on Instagram
  • Anthropic, PBC — to generate reply text via the Claude API. Anthropic does not retain message data for training per their commercial policy.
  • Railway, Inc. — for application hosting

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by blocking our Instagram account

To exercise these rights, contact us via Instagram DM or at the address below.

10. Children

Our content is intended for adults age 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has interacted with our account, please notify us immediately and we will delete their data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: contact us via direct message on @lexiloverlee.
